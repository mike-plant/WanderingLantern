## About This Event

Join us for our Family Classics Book Club, held on the **third Tuesday of every month**! This month, we'll be reading and discussing *The Legend of Sleepy Hollow* by Washington Irving.

### About the Book

In a quiet village along the Hudson River, superstitious schoolteacher Ichabod Crane encounters the terrifying Headless Horseman. Perfect for October! This spooky American classic blends humor, folklore, and spine-tingling suspense.

### What to Expect

- **Group Discussion**: Share your thoughts and favorite moments from the book
- **Family-Friendly Conversation**: Parents and children discuss together
- **Literary Exploration**: Learn about the book's themes, characters, and American folklore
- **Community Building**: Connect with other families who love reading

Please register so we can prepare adequate seating and materials for discussion!
