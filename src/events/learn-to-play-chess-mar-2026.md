## About This Event

Join us for a short introductory chess lesson followed by time to practice and play each other as you learn to play! Bring a chess set if you have one, or borrow one of ours. This is a free event, open to all ages — and for more experienced players, we'll also have a mini tournament with prizes!

### What's Happening

- **Short intro lesson** covering the rules and how each piece moves — with illustrated flashcards to help younger and newer players pick it up fast
- **Open play time** to practice with other kids
- **Mini tournament** for older or more experienced players — skill levels will be grouped, with prizes for the winners!
- **Friendly, welcoming atmosphere** — all skill levels welcome
- **Chess sets provided** — or bring your own if you have one!

### Details

- **Free event** — no cost to attend
- **All ages welcome** — parents use their best judgment
- Bring a chess set if you have one, or borrow one of ours

Please register so we can make sure we have enough chess sets and space for everyone!
