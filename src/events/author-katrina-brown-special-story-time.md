Join us on July 18th for a special author visit with Katrina Brown, author of _Dancing with Kayden_.

Katrina will share her heartfelt children’s book about Kayden, a young boy who may not use many words, but expresses himself beautifully through rhythm, movement, and dance. With the love and support of his family, teacher, and music therapy group, Kayden discovers confidence, joy, and his own wonderful way of communicating with the world.

This special storytime celebrates music, movement, self-expression, and the many different ways children connect, communicate, and shine. Families are invited to come listen, move, and enjoy a meaningful morning with a story that reminds us that every child has something beautiful to say.
