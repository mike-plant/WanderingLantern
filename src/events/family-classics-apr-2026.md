## About This Event

Join us for our Family Classics Book Club, held on the **third Tuesday of every month**! This month, we'll be reading and discussing *Winnie the Pooh* by A.A. Milne.

### About the Book

Enter the Hundred Acre Wood and meet the beloved bear of very little brain, along with Piglet, Eeyore, Tigger, and Christopher Robin. These gentle, whimsical stories about friendship and adventure have charmed readers for generations.

### What to Expect

- **Group Discussion**: Share your thoughts and favorite moments from the book
- **Family-Friendly Conversation**: Parents and children discuss together
- **Literary Exploration**: Learn about the book's themes, characters, and timeless wisdom
- **Community Building**: Connect with other families who love reading

Please register so we can prepare adequate seating and materials for discussion!
