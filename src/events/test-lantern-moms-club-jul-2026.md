## About This Event

If you believe in the power of books, community, and raising readers, this evening is for you. The **Lantern Moms Club** is a relaxed, no-pressure get-together hosted by The Wandering Lantern team — a chance to hear about what's happening at the store, meet other moms in the neighborhood, and find your place in the literary community growing right here in Lakewood.

### What to Expect

Come as you are. This is a casual evening designed for connection — a warm, cozy space with friendly faces where you can:

- **Learn more** about the events, programming, and community happenings at The Wandering Lantern
- **Meet like-minded moms** who care about reading, creativity, and living a little more intentionally
- **Be part of something growing** — a neighborhood community built around stories, shared attention, and family life

### About The Wandering Lantern

The Wandering Lantern is more than a bookstore — it's a gathering place for families in Lakewood. Co-owner Emily Mitchell-Polci, an elementary school teacher, envisioned a store that helps families "build a community around reading aloud, creativity and shared attention, starting in the home." Alongside co-owner Michael Plant, she's brought that vision to life, and the Lakewood community has embraced it warmly.

The store already hosts weekly story times, craft nights, a family classics book club, and more — and this moms club is a natural extension of that spirit.
