## About This Event

Join us for our Family Classics Book Club, held on the **third Tuesday of every month**! This month, we'll be reading and discussing *Stuart Little* by E.B. White.

### About the Book

Stuart Little is a mouse born to a human family in New York City. Despite his small size, he embarks on big adventures - sailing toy boats, driving miniature cars, and searching for his friend Margalo the bird. A charming tale of courage and determination.

### What to Expect

- **Group Discussion**: Share your thoughts and favorite moments from the book
- **Family-Friendly Conversation**: Parents and children discuss together
- **Literary Exploration**: Learn about the book's themes, characters, and E.B. White's storytelling
- **Community Building**: Connect with other families who love reading
