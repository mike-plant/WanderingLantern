## The Biggest Bookstore Day of the Year

Every year, independent bookstores across the country celebrate together — and this year, we're going all out. Doors open at **10am** and close at **8pm**. Here's what's happening:

### Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| **10:00 AM** | Doors open early |
| **11:00 AM – 2:00 PM** | Story Karaoke |
| **11:00 AM – 12:30 PM** | Local Author Signing — Abby Dosen |
| **11:00 AM – 1:00 PM** | Face Painting |
| **3:30 PM** | Literary Cleveland Poetry Crawl |
| **4:30 PM** | Lakewood Music Academy Demo |
| **8:00 PM** | Closing time |

### All Day

- **Raffle** — enter to win throughout the day
- **Scavenger Hunt** — explore the store to find clues
- **Craft Activity** — drop in and make something
- **Vendor Displays** — local makers and community partners

No tickets, no registration — just come in and celebrate.
