## About This Event

Join us for an exciting afternoon with award-winning Cleveland author **Sarah Jenkins** as she shares her latest middle-grade adventure novel, *The Lantern Keepers*.

### Event Program

- **Author Reading**: Sarah reads an exclusive excerpt from her new book
- **Q&A Session**: Young readers can ask questions about writing, inspiration, and the creative process
- **Book Signing**: Purchase signed copies of Sarah's books
- **Writing Workshop**: Sarah shares tips for young writers

### About the Author

Sarah Jenkins is a Cleveland native and author of the beloved *Mystery Cove* series. Her books have been recognized with the Ohioana Book Award and have inspired countless young readers across the state.

### Registration

This event is free to attend, though registration is appreciated to help us prepare adequate seating. Books will be available for purchase at the event.
