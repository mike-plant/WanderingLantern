The Kindness Club — a cozy hour of stories with books by Barb Consiglio,  and also including simple kindness activities, and connection for young children and their caregivers. Through story time and play, we’ll explore empathy, helping others, and the small moments where kindness grows.  

**About Barb and Joy Jean Media:**

Barb is a mom of two young boys and the founder of Joy Jean Media, where she creates children’s books and parent resources designed to help families raise kind, empathetic kids through simple, everyday moments.

With a background as a health reporter, Barb brings a research-informed approach to parenting—translating what we know about child development into tools families can actually use. Her Kindness Series includes stories that help children understand empathy, helpfulness, and inclusion, each with “Kindness Conversation Starters” to carry the message beyond the page.

If you’re looking for meaningful stories and activities that also helps spark real conversations, you can explore the Kindness Series and join the Kindness Community (with a free 5-minute kindness habit guide) here: [www.JoyJeanMedia.com](http://www.joyjeanmedia.com/)
