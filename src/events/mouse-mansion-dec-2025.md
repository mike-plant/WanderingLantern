## About This Event

Come help us build the furniture for our in-store **Mouse Mansion** to be a part of the fun! Build and paint little die-cut wooden furniture that will remain in the store for the community to see and play with when they visit!

### What You'll Do

- **Build Miniature Furniture**: Assemble tiny wooden furniture pieces
- **Paint and Decorate**: Add your personal touch with paint and details
- **Become Part of the Store**: Your creation will be displayed in our permanent Mouse Mansion
- **Community Project**: Work alongside other families to create something special
- **Snacks Provided**: Enjoy refreshments while you craft

### Leave Your Mark

Put your personal touch on a piece of the store! Every child who participates will help create the miniature world that will delight visitors for years to come.
