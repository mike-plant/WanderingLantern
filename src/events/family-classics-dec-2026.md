## About This Event

Join us for our Family Classics Book Club, held on the **third Tuesday of every month**! This month, we'll be reading and discussing *The Lion, The Witch, and The Wardrobe* by C.S. Lewis.

### About the Book

Four siblings discover the magical land of Narnia through a wardrobe, where they join Aslan the lion in the battle against the White Witch's eternal winter. Perfect for December! A timeless fantasy adventure about courage, sacrifice, and redemption.

### What to Expect

- **Group Discussion**: Share your thoughts and favorite moments from the book
- **Family-Friendly Conversation**: Parents and children discuss together
- **Literary Exploration**: Learn about the book's themes, characters, and fantasy world-building
- **Community Building**: Connect with other families who love reading

Please register so we can prepare adequate seating and materials for discussion!
