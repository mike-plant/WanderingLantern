## About This Event

Join us for a special afternoon of live music at The Wandering Lantern! Noted local singer-songwriter **Congito Jaffe** will perform with acoustic vocals and guitar styling perfect for a cozy bookstore setting.

### About the Artist

Congito Jaffe is a longtime Lakewood resident of 25 years and former manager of InfoPLACE, the adult career resource center of Cuyahoga County Public Library, where he served for over 20 years. In 2025, Congito performed a well-received series of concerts for the Cuyahoga County Library system, played by invitation at many local coffeehouses and bookstores, and performed at a hurricane relief concert in Lansing, North Carolina.
