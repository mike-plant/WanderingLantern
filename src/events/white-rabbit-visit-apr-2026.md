## Down the Rabbit Hole (and Into the Bookstore)

The day before Easter, a very special visitor is making a stop at The Wandering Lantern — the **White Rabbit** in person, ready for photos and hugs.

### Schedule

- **11:00 AM - 12:00 PM** — Storytime! We'll read some of our favorite bunny stories together.
- **12:00 PM - 2:00 PM** — Visit and photo op with the White Rabbit. Bring your camera!

### Details

- **Free and open to all ages** — just drop in!
- No registration required.
- The perfect way to kick off your Easter weekend.
