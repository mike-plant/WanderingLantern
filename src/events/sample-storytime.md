## About This Event

Get into the holiday spirit with a special storytime celebration! We'll read beloved holiday classics and new favorites, then create a special craft to take home.

### What to Expect

- **Interactive Storytime**: Engaging read-alouds of seasonal picture books
- **Holiday Craft**: Create a festive decoration to take home
- **Snacks & Hot Cocoa**: Light refreshments provided
- **Photo Opportunity**: Capture memories in our cozy reading nook

### What to Bring

Please dress comfortably for crafting activities. All materials will be provided.

### Registration

Space is limited to 15 children to ensure an intimate, engaging experience. Please register below to secure your spot. This event is designed for ages 3-7, but siblings are welcome!
