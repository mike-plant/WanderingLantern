## About This Event

Join us for our Family Classics Book Club, held on the **third Tuesday of every month**! This month, we'll be reading and discussing *Pollyanna* by Eleanor H. Porter.

### About the Book

When young Pollyanna comes to live with her stern Aunt Polly, she brings with her one extraordinary gift: the ability to find something to be glad about in every situation. Her irrepressible optimism transforms everyone around her in ways no one expected. A beloved classic that has charmed readers for over a century.

### What to Expect

- **Group Discussion**: Share your thoughts and favorite moments from the book
- **Family-Friendly Conversation**: Parents and children discuss together
- **Literary Exploration**: Learn about the book's themes, characters, and lasting cultural impact
- **Community Building**: Connect with other families who love reading
