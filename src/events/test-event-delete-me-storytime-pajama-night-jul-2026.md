## About This Event

There's something extra special about hearing a great story in your pajamas. Join us at The Wandering Lantern for a cozy evening storytime — kids are encouraged to come dressed in their PJs, ready to settle in and listen.

Together we'll read **three picture books** by the lantern's glow, and warm up with **hot cocoa** for everyone.

### What to Expect

This relaxed, welcoming storytime is designed for children ages **3 to 8**. The evening setting and cozy atmosphere make it a wonderful way to wind down — and to share the joy of stories together.

### Details

- **Ages:** 3–8
- **Attire:** Pajamas encouraged!
