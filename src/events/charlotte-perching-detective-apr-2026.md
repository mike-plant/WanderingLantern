## A Storytime Like No Other

Get ready for a *very* special Saturday — author **Kristine Sheppard** is visiting The Wandering Lantern with her book *Charlotte the Perching Detective*, and she's bringing the real Charlotte along!

Charlotte is a golden Cocker Spaniel who has trained to do incredible things, including **scent work** — where she sniffs out hidden objects like a true detective. Come see her in action and help put her skills to the test.

### What to Expect

- **Meet Charlotte** — Kristine introduces Charlotte, shows off her training, and explains how she learned to "perch" and do scent work
- **Storytime** — Kristine reads *Charlotte the Perching Detective*, the story of how Charlotte learned to follow her nose
- **Live demo** — Kid volunteers hide scent tins around the store for Charlotte to find
- **Your turn!** — Kids get to give Charlotte treats and try to get her to do her tricks: paw, roll over, spin, and jump

### Books & More

Kristine will have copies of *Charlotte the Perching Detective* available to purchase, plus coloring books, bookmarks, sticker sheets, dog-shaped crayons, and a card game for dog trick training. Free coloring sheets for all kids!

This is a free drop-in event during our regular Saturday Story Time slot. All ages welcome — the littlest ones will love Charlotte, and the older kids will be amazed by her detective skills.
