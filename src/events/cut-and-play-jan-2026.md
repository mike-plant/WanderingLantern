## About This Event

Cut out the artistically rendered, articulating characters from a fairy tale to make the story come to life! Crafting and stories together thanks to artist **Emma R-C** from [@steelandstitch](https://instagram.com/steelandstitch).

### What to Expect

- **Story Time**: Listen to a classic fairy tale brought to life
- **Cut and Craft**: Create your own articulating paper characters
- **Take Home Fun**: Bring your characters home to continue the story
- **Artistic Guidance**: Learn techniques from a professional artist
- **All Supplies Included**: Everything you need is provided

### About the Artist

Emma R-C is a local Cleveland artist specializing in paper crafts and illustration. Her unique cut-and-play characters combine storytelling with hands-on creativity.
