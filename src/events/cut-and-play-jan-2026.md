## About This Event

Join Emily for an evening of storytelling and hands-on creativity! Kids will learn to make articulating paper characters that bring fairy tales to life, using sustainable craft materials designed by **Emma Friedlander-Collins** from [@steelandstitch](https://instagram.com/steelandstitch).

### What to Expect

- **Story Time**: Listen to a classic fairy tale brought to life
- **Cut and Craft**: Create articulating paper characters that move and play
- **Take Home Fun**: Continue the creative fun at home with simple supplies
- **Learn Skills**: Master techniques you can replicate with everyday household items
- **All Supplies Included**: Everything provided for the event

These beautiful cut-and-play characters are designed by **Emma Friedlander-Collins** ([@steelandstitch](https://instagram.com/steelandstitch)), a published author of multiple craft books and contributor to Mollie Makes and Simply Crochet magazines. Using eco-friendly materials, kids will learn sustainable crafting skills they can use again and again.
