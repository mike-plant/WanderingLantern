## About This Event

Join us for our Family Classics Book Club, held on the **third Tuesday of every month**! This month, we'll be reading and discussing *The Family Under the Bridge* by Natalie Savage Carlson.

### About the Book

Set in Paris, this heartwarming tale follows Armand, a carefree hobo who lives under a bridge and values his freedom above all else. His life changes when he reluctantly befriends three children and their mother, discovering that family and love matter more than independence.

### What to Expect

- **Group Discussion**: Share your thoughts and favorite moments from the book
- **Family-Friendly Conversation**: Parents and children discuss together
- **Literary Exploration**: Learn about the book's themes, characters, and historical context
- **Community Building**: Connect with other families who love reading
