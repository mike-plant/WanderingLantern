Blast off with us for a special author visit with Joshua Galang, author of _Rocket Trip: The Solar System Search_!

Joshua is an author, illustrator, educator, and experience designer who loves using technology, art, and storytelling to help kids learn. During this interactive visit, he’ll read from _Rocket Trip_, an out-of-this-world adventure about three kids who journey through the solar system with a robotic friend on a mission to rescue a stranded astronaut.

After the story, Joshua will talk with kids about space, creativity, and STEAM—science, technology, engineering, art, and math—and how imagination can help us explore big ideas. Perfect for curious kids, young inventors, future astronauts, and anyone who loves a good adventure.
