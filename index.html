About Us

The Wandering Lantern is a community bookstore created by Emily and Michael, two book lovers who wanted to bring something special to Lakewood.

Emily Mitchell is the heart of our book curation. With a Master's in English Literature and a background in teaching, she has a genuine love for children's stories and helping young readers discover books they'll treasure. As a mom of four and former homeschool educator, she knows firsthand how the right book at the right time can make all the difference.

Michael Plant is a local realtor and small business owner who believes thriving neighborhoods need gathering places where people connect. He brings business operations and community relationships to the table, working to make The Wandering Lantern a welcoming space for Lakewood families.
